Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

What is a cloud readiness assessment?

A structured evaluation of your current infrastructure, applications, and data to determine the optimal cloud migration strategy. Parkar's assessment delivers a workload disposition for every application, a migration roadmap, TCO model, and landing zone recommendation — typically completed in 2 weeks.

What is FinOps and why is it important?

FinOps is the practice of bringing financial accountability to cloud spending. It combines real-time cost visibility, usage optimisation, and budget governance. Without FinOps, enterprises typically overspend on cloud by 30–40% due to idle resources, over-provisioned instances, and lack of cost attribution.

How do you handle multi-cloud environments?

We design cloud foundations across Azure, AWS, and GCP based on workload requirements — not vendor preference. Each hyperscaler has strengths: Azure for enterprise integration, AWS for breadth of services, GCP for data and AI workloads. Our landing zone architecture supports hybrid and multi-cloud by design.

What security controls are included in your cloud deployments?

Every cloud foundation includes identity and access management, network segmentation, encryption at rest and in transit, CSPM (Cloud Security Posture Management), compliance guardrails for HIPAA/SOC2/PCI-DSS, and continuous monitoring via Vector.
