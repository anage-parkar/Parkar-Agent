Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

What is agentic AI and how is it different from copilots?

Agentic AI refers to autonomous agents that can reason, decide, and execute multi-step workflows across enterprise systems without continuous human prompting. Unlike copilots that assist with individual tasks, agentic AI operates end-to-end — connecting data, applications, and business logic to complete complex processes independently, with governance and human oversight built in.

How long does it take to deploy agentic AI in production?

With Parkar's AIONIQ platform, enterprise teams can move from AI readiness assessment to production-ready agentic workflows in weeks, not the typical 12–18 months. Our structured approach includes use-case prioritisation, data readiness, multi-agent orchestration, governance, and managed operations from day one.

What governance controls does Parkar build into agentic AI?

Every agentic AI deployment includes role-based access control, full audit trails, human-in-the-loop approval workflows, MCP gateway integration for tool access governance, and Cedar policy enforcement. These controls are embedded from the first sprint, not added as an afterthought.

Which industries benefit most from agentic AI?

Financial services (compliance automation, fraud detection), healthcare (clinical workflows, population health), manufacturing (predictive maintenance, supply chain), and high-tech (developer productivity, SaaS operations) see the fastest ROI from agentic AI deployments.
