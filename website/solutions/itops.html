Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

What is AIOps and how does it reduce alert fatigue?

AIOps uses AI and machine learning to correlate, deduplicate, and prioritise IT alerts from multiple monitoring tools. Instead of thousands of raw alerts, AIOps surfaces 50–120 actionable incidents per day. Parkar's Vector platform delivers 65%+ reduction in alert noise and 40%+ improvement in MTTR.

What is the difference between a NOC and a SOC?

A Network Operations Centre (NOC) monitors infrastructure availability, performance, and uptime. A Security Operations Centre (SOC) focuses on threat detection, incident response, and security compliance. Parkar operates both as managed services, often integrated for unified visibility.

How do managed IT services work with Parkar?

We take full operational ownership of your IT environment — ITSM, AIOps monitoring, NOC, security operations, and 24/7 managed services. Your internal team is freed to focus on strategic initiatives while we handle the operational load with defined SLAs and continuous improvement.

What ITSM platforms do you support?

We implement and manage ServiceNow, Freshservice, and BMC Helix. ServiceNow is our primary platform for enterprise ITSM, with deep expertise in CMDB, change management, incident management, and service catalogue design.
