Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

When should an enterprise modernise vs rebuild an application?

Modernise when the core business logic is sound but the infrastructure is outdated — typically monoliths that need decomposition into microservices, or on-premise apps moving to cloud. Rebuild when the application architecture fundamentally cannot support AI, real-time data, or modern user expectations.

What is platform engineering?

Platform engineering is the discipline of building and maintaining internal developer platforms that standardise how teams build, deploy, and operate software. It reduces cognitive load on developers, enforces best practices, and accelerates delivery — typically cutting deployment time by 50–70%.

How do you embed AI into existing enterprise applications?

We integrate AI capabilities directly into application workflows — not as bolt-on features. This includes AI-powered search, intelligent automation of manual processes, predictive analytics embedded in dashboards, and agentic workflows that execute business logic autonomously.

What hyperscalers do you support for application modernisation?

We build on Azure, AWS, and GCP — in any language and framework your teams work in. Our approach is hyperscaler-agnostic, selecting the right platform based on your existing estate, compliance requirements, and workload characteristics.
