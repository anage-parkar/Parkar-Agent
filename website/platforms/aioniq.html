Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

What is AIONIQ?

AIONIQ is Parkar's enterprise operating model for agentic AI. It provides a structured path from fragmented AI pilots to governed, production-ready agentic workflows — connecting data, apps, cloud, and operations into one reliable AI execution engine with governance, observability, and human oversight built in.

How is AIONIQ different from building AI in-house?

Building AI in-house typically takes 12–18 months and most pilots never reach production. AIONIQ provides pre-built agent templates, data connectors, governance frameworks, and deployment pipelines that compress this timeline to weeks. You get production-ready AI without building the entire infrastructure from scratch.

Is AIONIQ SOC2 certified?

Yes. AIONIQ is SOC2 certified, ensuring enterprise-grade security, availability, and confidentiality controls. It also supports HIPAA compliance for healthcare workloads and includes built-in audit trails, role-based access, and data governance controls.

What does the AIONIQ commercial model look like?

Parkar invests Months 1–2 at no cost, building your AI readiness assessment and first agent workflows. Billing starts only when a production agent is live and delivering measurable value. You pay for results, not process.
