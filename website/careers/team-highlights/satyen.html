Team Highlights / Satyen
Consultant Spotlight

Satyen

Parkar Team Member

Don't be afraid to give up the good to go for the great.

Q What was your childhood like?

As a kid, I was crazy about cricket. Summer breaks meant one thing: non-stop tournaments where I'd spend every moment perfecting my batting. Now, even with grown-up responsibilities, I still sneak in some cricket on weekends, keeping the childhood joy alive.

Q What is that one quality that has helped you along your career path?

Believe in yourself. Having a clear goal is essential throughout your journey. Keeping a positive mindset is key as you chase your dreams. Trust in your ability to overcome any challenges that come along the way because you've got what it takes to succeed.

Q What do you appreciate most about Parkar?

In Parkar, it's all about teamwork, support, and respect. Everyone pitches in, no questions asked, creating a tight-knit community where collaboration is second nature. This friendly vibe makes Parkar a place where unity and camaraderie thrive, making everyone feel right at home.

Q Do you feel unstoppable at Parkar?

I started my career with Parkar as a Graduate Technical Engineer (GTE), and over the years, I have had invaluable opportunities to grow and excel in this company. Their trust boosted my confidence and gave me the freedom to take charge and make real impacts on the projects. People at Parkar made me believe that I can do anything.

Q What advice would you give to someone who just started their career?

For someone just starting, explore and experiment, and don't be afraid to think outside the box. Setting big goals is key — let them light up your path and push you to reach new heights.
