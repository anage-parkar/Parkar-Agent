Our Journey

Milestones of Progress

Key moments that have shaped Parkar into the enterprise technology partner it is today.

2016

Parkar Founded

In 2016, Parkar was established with the vision of unlocking workplace potential and driving meaningful enterprise transformation across industries. What started as a small team with a big idea has grown into a global technology partner.

Expanding Capabilities

By 2017, Parkar had expanded its practice areas to include data analytics and cloud modernization — establishing a multi-discipline delivery model that continues to define our offerings today.

2017
2018

Global Growth

Parkar opened its US headquarters in Atlanta, Georgia, establishing a bi-continental delivery model that allowed us to serve enterprise clients across North America and South Asia with seamless continuity.

Accelerating at Scale

By 2020, Parkar had onboarded over 100,000 active users across client platforms and had established deep partnerships with Microsoft, AWS, and Google Cloud — cementing our position as a trusted enterprise transformation partner.

2020

One Team, Global Impact
