You choose the scope. Whether you need only Data Engineering, a Cloud migration, or a full end-to-end Agentic AI deployment, our practices work as standalone engagements or as one integrated programme. Many clients start with one area and expand over time — 95% of our clients return for the next programme.

Which industries do you specialise in?

We have deep domain context in Financial Services, Healthcare & Life Sciences, Manufacturing & Industrial, and High-Tech & SaaS. This matters because the data models, compliance requirements, and AI use cases are fundamentally different across sectors — and generic approaches rarely ship to production in regulated environments.

What are AIONIQ and Vector, and do I need both?

AIONIQ is our AI operating model platform — it governs everything from AI readiness to production agentic workflows. Vector is our intelligent IT operations and observability layer that keeps your cloud, apps, and AI environments reliable and cost-efficient. They're designed to work together, but each delivers standalone value. We'll recommend the right fit based on your priorities.

Is Parkar's AI platform secure and compliant?

Yes. AIONIQ is SOC 2 certified, and our practices are built for regulated enterprise environments — including HIPAA-compliant data governance for Healthcare and risk & compliance automation for Financial Services. Security and governance are built in, not bolted on.

Which cloud platforms and technology partners do you work with?

We're platform-agnostic and partner with the leading global providers — Microsoft Azure, AWS, Google Cloud, Snowflake, Databricks, Salesforce, Tableau, Atlassian, and ScienceLogic among others. Our certified practitioners work across every layer of the modern enterprise stack.
