Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

How does AI improve patient outcomes in healthcare?

AI improves outcomes through population health analytics (identifying at-risk patients before acute episodes), clinical decision support (real-time recommendations based on patient data), operational efficiency (reducing wait times and readmissions), and interoperability (connecting fragmented health data into unified patient views).

Is your healthcare platform HIPAA compliant?

Yes. Every healthcare solution we build is HIPAA compliant by design — with PHI encryption, access controls, audit logging, and data governance embedded at the infrastructure layer, not bolted on after deployment. We also support HITRUST and SOC2 compliance frameworks.

What is population health analytics?

Population health analytics uses data from multiple sources — EHRs, claims, social determinants, IoT devices — to identify health trends, predict risk, and intervene proactively across patient populations. Parkar builds these analytics platforms on Databricks and Azure with FHIR integration for healthcare interoperability.
