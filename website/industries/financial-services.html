How does AI help with fraud detection in financial services?

AI enables real-time fraud detection by analysing transaction patterns, device behaviour, and customer risk profiles simultaneously — catching sophisticated fraud that rule-based systems miss. Parkar builds connected fraud data architectures that reduce false positives by 50–70% while improving detection rates.

Is Parkar's platform compliant with financial regulations?

Yes. Our solutions are built for regulated environments with SOC2, PCI-DSS, and GDPR compliance controls embedded from the architecture layer. We have 10+ years of experience delivering in financial services across lending, payments, wealth management, and insurance.

What is intelligent lending and how does AI improve it?

Intelligent lending uses AI to automate credit decisioning, risk assessment, and loan processing — reducing approval times from days to minutes while improving risk accuracy. Parkar integrates AI models directly into lending workflows with full audit trails and explainability.
