Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

How do you help SaaS companies scale faster?

We accelerate SaaS growth through platform engineering (standardised developer platforms), application modernisation (decomposing monoliths), AI-driven operations (automated scaling and incident response), and data engineering (product analytics and customer intelligence) — all designed to help engineering teams ship faster.

What is platform engineering for high-tech companies?

Platform engineering builds internal developer platforms that abstract away infrastructure complexity. Engineers get self-service deployment, standardised CI/CD pipelines, and pre-configured environments — reducing onboarding from months to days and deployment frequency from weekly to multiple times per day.

How does AI improve developer productivity?

AI improves developer productivity through intelligent code review, automated testing, AI-assisted debugging, and custom copilots built for your specific codebase and workflows. Parkar builds these capabilities as embedded tools within your existing development environment, not standalone products.
