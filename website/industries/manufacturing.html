Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

What is predictive maintenance and how does it reduce downtime?

Predictive maintenance uses IoT sensor data and machine learning to predict equipment failures before they happen — replacing reactive break-fix with proactive intervention. Manufacturers using predictive maintenance typically see 90% reduction in unplanned downtime and 25–30% reduction in maintenance costs.

How does AI improve supply chain operations?

AI optimises supply chains through demand forecasting (predicting inventory needs), route optimisation (reducing logistics costs), supplier risk assessment (identifying vulnerabilities), and real-time visibility (tracking materials across the entire chain). Parkar builds these capabilities on Azure and Databricks.

What is a smart factory?

A smart factory uses connected IoT devices, real-time data analytics, and AI-driven automation to optimise manufacturing operations. This includes predictive quality control, automated production scheduling, energy optimisation, and digital twin simulations — all connected through a unified data platform.
